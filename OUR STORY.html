Our Brand Journey

In 2018, fueled by a passion for health and wellness, a man with a background in managing warehouses took a bold step. Leaving behind the boardroom, he returned to Pakistan from Australia with a dream and family support. The first Shrek Juices opened in Giga Mall, offering pure refreshments with no sugar, water, or preservatives. Its success sparked a nationwide expansion, with stores in Rawalpindi, Islamabad, Peshawar, and more.

In a nation where fast food dominates, Shrek Juices provides a healthier alternative—fresh fruits, vegetables, smoothies, and protein shakes. Today, Shrek Juices is a source of hope for a healthier Pakistan and beyond. Join our movement as we rewrite the narrative of health, one sip at a time.

Our Vision

Our vision at Shrek Juices is a bold declaration of intent, paving the path toward a healthier, happier Pakistan. We aspire to dominance—to become the undisputed titan of the juice industry, stretching our reach far and wide across the length and breadth of this great nation. Yet, our vision extends beyond mere market supremacy; it is a rallying cry, urging consumers to embrace a lifestyle defined by vitality and balance.

Our Mission

Commencing this journey, we have set our sights on horizons far beyond our borders. Our mission is our commitment to spreading the gospel of health and wellness to every corner of the globe. In 2024, we took our first step toward international acclaim with the opening of our inaugural store in the United Kingdom. And yet, this is just the beginning. Over the next five years, we will continue to chart a course toward global domination. For at Shrek Juices, our mission is not merely to serve; it is to inspire—one sip at a time.
